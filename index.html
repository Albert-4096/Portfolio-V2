04 About

I'm Albert, 18, from Romania. I got into AI through neuroscience — I wanted to understand how brains work and ended up training the artificial kind. That led to machine learning, which led to security, which led to a rack server at home running 95 containers.

I compete in CTFs and in the Romanian AI & Cybersecurity Olympiad at the national level. University for AI is next. I'm endlessly curious, always diving into anything I find interesting or useful — lately that includes 3D art and photography, which can keep me away from a terminal just long enough.
